The Contemporary Journal

The Contemporary Journal is the web journal of the Public Programmes and Research at Nottingham Contemporary. An Open Access publishing and research platform that further explores the programme research strands; widens and transnatinalises its research community; and brings together interdisciplinary modes of enquiry in the fields of artistic research, the curatorial and visual cultures.
