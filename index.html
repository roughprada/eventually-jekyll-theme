Discourse analysis for the campaign
of Senator Jorge Robledo
(private)

We gathered an actionable mapping of concepts used by the Colombian Senator in his key public speeches. This map helped us visualise the public rhetorical field of the candidate as a whole, and point to strengths and areas for improvement in his communicative address. The results were transformed into insights for the communication design of his political campaign and for the taking of strategic decisions.
